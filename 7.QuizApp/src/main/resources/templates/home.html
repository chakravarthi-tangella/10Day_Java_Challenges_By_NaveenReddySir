Welcome To Quiz App

  • Welcome to the Quiz App!

  • Choose between Admin or User login options on the home page.

  • Admin:
    • Admins have access to powerful features to create, manage, and verify quizzes.
    • Create captivating quizzes by adding questions, options, and correct answers.
    • Update, delete, and view all questions to maintain the quiz database efficiently.

  • User:
    • Log in as a User to explore a vast collection of quizzes created by Admins.
    • Enter the technology name to discover relevant quizzes.
    • Engage in exciting quiz sessions and challenge your intellect.
    • Receive immediate feedback on the number of correct answers.
    • Track your progress and improve your knowledge.

  • Powered by Spring Boot, JPA, and Thymeleaf for a seamless experience.
  • Elegant design and user-friendly interface for easy navigation.
  • Reliable performance and efficient data management.
  • Embark on an educational journey and unlock your potential with the Quiz App!
